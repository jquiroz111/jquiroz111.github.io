Turn Old Laptop Display Into External Monitor

Materials needed:


  • Old unused laptop
  • Controller board that works with display
  • Video cable
  • Frame (Optional)

Making The External Display

  1. Disassemble the display from the laptop.

  2. Once the display is disassembled look for the serial number in the back of the display.
  3. You will need this information in order to get the correct controller board.

  4. Once you have the controller board, simply plug it in to the display

  5. Your new display is ready to go!

  6. Optional: You can choose to build a frame for the display and/or conroller board.
  7. Depending on the model of your display you may find 3D printing files online that will do the trick.
